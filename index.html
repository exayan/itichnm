About CHNM Trade

Computer Hardware & Network Maintenance (CHNM) is a one-year trade under the Craftsmen Training Scheme, affiliated for Govt ITI Anantnag with an intake of 24 trainees.

Course Structure

  • Duration: 1 Year (2 semesters)
  • Level: NSQF Aligned
  • Intake: 24 trainees (single unit, one shift)
  • Mode: Classroom + Lab-based practical training

Eligibility & Outcomes

  • Eligibility: Generally 10th pass from recognized board
  • Focus: Hardware maintenance, basic networking, OS support
  • Job roles: Computer hardware technician, IT support, field technician, lab assistant
  • Higher studies: Advanced hardware, networking, diploma courses

Trade Overview – CHNM

The Computer Hardware & Network Maintenance trade trains students to assemble, maintain, and troubleshoot personal computers and basic networks. Trainees learn to work with motherboards, storage devices, RAM, power supplies, operating systems, and network components like switches and cabling.

On successful completion, students can work as hardware technicians, IT support staff, or pursue higher studies in networking and system administration.

Teaching Philosophy – Mr. Zahoor

Mr. Zahoor believes that hardware and networking can only be mastered through consistent hands-on practice. His approach combines clear theory notes, live demonstrations on real systems, and step-by-step troubleshooting.

Students are encouraged to document every practical, understand the root cause of faults, and develop confidence in handling systems independently. Discipline, punctuality, and respect for lab equipment are strongly emphasized.
